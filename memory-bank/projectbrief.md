# Project Summary

The Sleep Wellness & Chronotype Intelligence Platform is a white-label SaaS platform designed to educate individuals and organizations about sleep, biological rhythms, chronotypes, and wellness.

The platform combines:

- Sleep Education
- Sleep Disorder Awareness
- Chronotype Assessment
- Personalized Recommendations
- Corporate Wellness
- Research Analytics
- White Label Organization Management

The platform serves both individual users and organizations.

---

# Primary Goal

Help users understand:

- Their sleep patterns
- Their biological rhythm
- Their chronotype
- Their performance patterns
- Their wellness opportunities

The platform should transform assessment results into meaningful and actionable insights.

# Organization Model

Organizations receive:

- Unique Organization Account
- Unique Organization Code
- Shareable Assessment Link
- Admin Dashboard
- Analytics Access

Example:

website.com/ORG-7XQ2M8

Members joining through organization links are automatically associated with that organization.

# User Acquisition Types

Organization Member

Direct Member

Referral Member

---

# Assessment Philosophy

Assessment should be simple.

Users should not be forced to create an account before completing the assessment.

Preferred flow:

Details
↓
Assessment
↓
Result
↓
Report
↓
Optional Login

This reduces friction and increases completion rates.
