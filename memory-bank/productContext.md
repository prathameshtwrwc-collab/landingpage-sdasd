# Product Context

## Product Overview

The Sleep Wellness & Chronotype Intelligence Platform is a comprehensive wellness, assessment, education, analytics, and research platform.

The platform is not a simple questionnaire application.

The platform combines:

- Sleep Education
- Sleep Wellness
- Chronotype Assessment
- Personalized Recommendations
- Corporate Wellness
- Research Analytics
- White Label SaaS Infrastructure

The platform is designed to help individuals and organizations understand sleep patterns, biological rhythms, performance optimization, and overall wellness.

---

# Vision

To create a scalable platform that helps people understand how sleep and biological rhythms influence:

- Energy
- Performance
- Productivity
- Recovery
- Mood
- Health
- Lifestyle

The platform aims to bridge sleep science with practical daily life.
